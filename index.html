About the App

The CD Interest Calculator app lets you compute the maturity amount and total interest earned for any deposit amount, interest rate, and tenure. Choose from monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, or annual compounding and view a detailed growth schedule.

Features

- Simple, user-friendly interface
- Calculate maturity amounts instantly
- Supports different compounding frequencies
- View interest growth schedule over time
- Safe and does not collect personal data
